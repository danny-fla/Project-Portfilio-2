How To Play:

Good at geography?

Alright then, come and have a go!

The rules are easy.

  • To begin the quiz select Let's Go! on the Home Page
  • You will then need to create a username and select Begin
  • Once the quiz starts, you must match the landmark shown to the correct option below
  • Each correct answer will result in 10 points
  • But don't delay - there will be a 15 second timer for each question!

Think you're ready? Back to the Home Page to begin
